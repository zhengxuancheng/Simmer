Simmer · Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-20 · Last updated: 2026-04-20

Simmer ("we", "us", the "App") is an iOS application that helps you capture fleeting ideas and nurture them into actionable plans through AI-assisted conversation. This policy explains what data Simmer collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 On-device data

  • Your ideas: the text you capture, classification tags, energy scores, reminder schedules, conversation history, and your derived personal profile. This data is stored on your device using Apple SwiftData and optionally synchronized across your own devices via Apple iCloud (CloudKit).
  • Settings: language preference, notification schedule, quiet hours, profile-learning on/off toggle.

1.2 Data sent to third-party AI providers

When a conversation cannot be handled by Apple's on-device Foundation Models (iOS 26+ required for local inference), Simmer sends the specific text of that conversation turn to Google Gemini 2.5 Flash through our own relay on Cloudflare Workers. Specifically:

  • The relay URL is https://simmer-api.liujinxingde2008.workers.dev (edge-served, no backend database).
  • Only the minimum text needed to answer your message is sent. No user identifier, no device identifier, no IP-derived location.
  • Google is the model provider. Simmer does not store your conversation on the relay; the relay proxies the request and streams the answer back.
  • Google's handling of the forwarded text is governed by the Gemini API Terms of Service.

On devices running iOS 26 or later, Simmer will prefer Apple Foundation Models (local, offline, zero data transfer) for classification and short-text tasks. You cannot opt out of the local path; opting out of cloud AI will limit conversation length and reasoning quality.

1.3 Data we do NOT collect

  • No analytics SDKs, no crash reporting that identifies users, no advertising IDs.
  • No contact list, microphone recording archive, photo library, or location data.
  • No account system. Simmer does not have user accounts or passwords.

2. How Data Is Used

  • To render the list of your captured ideas and calculate energy levels locally.
  • To schedule notifications on your device.
  • To produce AI-assisted conversation and classification results for you, and only you.
  • If iCloud sync is enabled, to mirror your data across your own Apple ID's devices via Apple's CloudKit (Apple stores it encrypted on your behalf; Simmer developer has no access).

3. Data Retention & Deletion

  • All on-device data persists until you delete it.
  • You can clear your learned profile at any time: Settings → 画像 → 清除画像.
  • You can pause profile learning at any time: Settings → 画像 → 暂停学习.
  • Deleting the App from your device removes all local data. If iCloud sync is enabled, Apple's standard retention rules apply.
  • We do not retain your conversation content on our Cloudflare Worker beyond the duration of each request.

4. Children

Simmer is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the App or the law. We will post the updated policy at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

6. Contact

Questions or requests: liujinxingde2008@hotmail.com

Simmer · 隱私政策

生效日期：2026-04-20 · 最後更新：2026-04-20

Simmer（「我們」、「本應用」）是一款 iOS 應用，協助您捕捉一閃而過的想法，透過 AI 對話把它們煨成可行的方案。本政策說明 Simmer 收集哪些資料、如何使用，以及您擁有的選擇。

1. 我們收集的資料

1.1 設備本地資料

  • 您的想法：您輸入的文字、分類標籤、能量分數、提醒排程、對話記錄、以及從中提煉的個人畫像。這些資料透過 Apple SwiftData 儲存在您的設備上，並可選擇透過 Apple iCloud（CloudKit）在您自己的 Apple ID 設備間同步。
  • 設定：語言偏好、通知排程、勿擾時段、畫像學習開關。

1.2 傳送至第三方 AI 供應商的資料

當對話無法由 Apple 設備端 Foundation Models 處理時（需 iOS 26+ 才能本地推理），Simmer 會將該輪對話的文字透過我們部署於 Cloudflare Workers 的中繼，傳送到 Google Gemini 2.5 Flash。具體：

  • 中繼網址為 https://simmer-api.liujinxingde2008.workers.dev（邊緣節點，無後端資料庫）。
  • 僅傳送回答您本輪所需的最小文字。不附加使用者識別碼、設備識別碼或 IP 位置。
  • 模型提供方為 Google。Simmer 不在中繼端儲存您的對話，中繼只代理轉發並串流回應。
  • Google 對轉發文字的處理依其 Gemini API 服務條款 規範。

在 iOS 26 或更新版本的設備，Simmer 會優先使用 Apple Foundation Models（設備端、離線、零資料外傳）處理分類與短文本任務。您無法停用本地路徑；停用雲端 AI 會限制對話長度與推理品質。

1.3 我們不收集的資料

  • 沒有分析 SDK、沒有可識別使用者的崩潰回報、沒有廣告識別碼。
  • 沒有通訊錄、麥克風錄音歸檔、相簿、或定位資料。
  • 沒有帳戶系統。Simmer 沒有使用者帳號或密碼。

2. 資料如何被使用

  • 在本機呈現您捕捉的想法列表並計算能量分數。
  • 在您的設備上排程推送通知。
  • 為您（也僅為您）產生 AI 對話與分類結果。
  • 若啟用 iCloud 同步，透過 Apple CloudKit 在您自己 Apple ID 的設備之間鏡像資料（Apple 以加密方式替您保存，Simmer 開發者無存取權）。

3. 資料保留與刪除

  • 所有設備端資料會持續保留，直到您刪除為止。
  • 您隨時可以清除學到的畫像：設定 → 畫像 → 清除畫像。
  • 您隨時可以暫停畫像學習：設定 → 畫像 → 暫停學習。
  • 從設備刪除本應用會清除所有本機資料。若啟用 iCloud 同步，則依 Apple 的標準保留規則處理。
  • 我們不在 Cloudflare Worker 上保留您的對話內容，僅限請求期間。

4. 兒童

Simmer 非針對未滿 13 歲的兒童設計。我們不會在知情情況下收集兒童的個人資訊。
